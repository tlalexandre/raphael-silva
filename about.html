About me

Hello there, I am Raphael Silva, a multidisciplinary Art Director based in Dublin. Born in the nineties, I studied Graphic Design at Miami Ad School and Digital Design at ECDD, in Rio de Janeiro.

Creative agencies have been my second home for the past 7 years. Always working with amazing clients, such as Lego, Vodafone, Dropbox, Hennessy, and Guinness, among others.

Currently interested in human behaviour, sustainability, innovation, and interactive technologies. I am looking forward to matching animation with interactive projects.
